Welcome to the Peg Jump Game!

The objective of this game is to remove all pegs on the triangle except one.

Select a peg and move it diagonally over another to an open position on the board. The peg you jumped over will be removed. You cannot move a peg unless it jumps over another. Repeat until you have won the game, or until there no moves left.

Be careful - a couple of wrong moves can cost you the game!
